Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of honey wheat bread
  • 1 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice of colby jack cheese
  • 1 slice of swiss cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoon butter

Directions

Step 1:Heat frying pan on medium heat.

Step 2:Butter one side of both slices of bread and sprinkle with italian seasoning.

Step 3:Place one slice of bread butter slide first in the pan.

Step 4: Layer cheese on the bread.

Step 5: Add other slice of bread.

\

Step 6: Cook for about four minutes.

Step 7: Carefully flip sandwich and flatten with spatula.

Step 8: Cook until toast is golden brown on both sides.
