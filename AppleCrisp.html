Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 3/4 cup flour
  • 2 teaspoons cream of tartar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Directions

Step 1:Preheat oven to 400.

Step 2: Combine 1 1/2 cups sugar, butter, eggs, milk and vanilla. Beat with electric mixture.

Step 3: Mix flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Slowly add mixture to wet ingredient mixture until blended.

Step 4: Combine 1/4 cup sugar and cinnamon. Roll dough into 1-inch balls. Roll dough in cinnamon sugar. Place on baking sheet.

Step 5: Bake for 8-10 minutes.

Step 6: Let cool for 2 minutes
