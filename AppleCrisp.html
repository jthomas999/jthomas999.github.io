Apple Crisp

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup quick-cooking oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 Granny Smith apples, peeled and sliced

Directions

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350.

Step 2: Combine sugar, flour, cinnamon, quick-cooking oats, brown sugar and baking soda.

Step 3:Cover the bottom of a baking dish with the apple slices.

Step 4:Cover the apples with the dry mixture.

Step 5:Dot the top of the dry mixture with butter pieces.

Step 6: Bake for 25-30 mintues, until golden brown.
