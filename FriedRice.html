Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cooked chicken breasts
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 boil in bag white rice, chilled a
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli
  • 1 cup cooked carrots
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons teryaki sauce

Directions

Step 1:Boil rice in a pot of water for about 10 minutes.

Step 2: Cover the bottom of a frying pan with olive oil and heat on medium heat.

Step 3: Cut chicken into small pieces and cook until golden brown, set aside.

Step 4: Rinse pan and heat olive oil over medium heat.

Step 5: Remove rice from pan and chill in the fridge for about ten minutes

Step 6: Add broccoli, carrots, and onion to the pan with oilve oil.

Step 7:In the same pan with the vegetables fry and chopp up the eggs.

Step 8: Mix vegetables and eggs together and push to the side of the pan.

Step 9: Add rice, chicken, soy sauce and teryacki sauce to the pan

Step 10:Cook for a few minutes and then mix all ingridients together.

Step 11: Let ingredients cook together for a few mintues and then serve and enjoy!
