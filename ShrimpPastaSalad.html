Shrimp Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • 15-20 Raw Jumbo Shrimp
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic salt
  • 1 tablespoon Onion Powder
  • 1 box bowtie pasta
  • 15-20 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 Feta cheese crumbles
  • 1/2 red onion
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons italian seasoning
  • A pinch of salt and pepper

Directions

Step 1:Cook Pasta according to the dirextions on the box, set aside to cool.

Step 2:Peel shell and tails off of the shrimp.

Step 3:Toss shrimp in garlic salt and onion powder.

Step 4: Heat olive oil in frying pan over medium heat.

Step 5:Carefully place shrimp in the frying pan and cook for 3 minutes per side, set aside to cool.

Step 6: Chop onion and cut tomatoes, set aside

Step 7:Mix together red wine vinegar, olive oil, italian seasoning, salt and pepper

Step 8:Toss all ingriendents in a large mixing bowel

Step 9:Sprinkle with feta

Step 10:Chill pasta in the fridge for about 30 minutes.
