Sausage Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 package italian sausage
  • 1 can of tomato soup
  • 1 box angel hair pasta
  • 10-15 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 2 teasppoons oregano

Directions

Step 1: Fill a pot with water. Bring the water to a boil and add the pasta. Cook the pasta for 5 minutes.

Step 2: Cover the bottom of a frying pan with olive oil and heat on medium heat.

Step 3: Add 1/4 cup water to frying pan. Cook sausage in the pan with olive oil and water covered for 10-15 minutes

Step 4: In a seperate pan hehat olive oil on medium heat.

Step 5: Add spinach, onion, tomatoes and seasonings to this pan and let simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 6: Drain the pasta and return the pasta to the pot.

Step 7: Add the tomato soup to the pan with the vegetables and simmer for a few additional minutes.

Step 8: Cut the sausage into small pieces

Step 9: Add the pasta and the sausage to the pan with the vegetables and tomato soup and simmer for a few additional minutes.

Step 10: Serve and enjoy!
