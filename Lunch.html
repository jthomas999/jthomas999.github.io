Grilled Turkey Panini

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of italian bread
  • 2 slices of deli turkey
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 slice of tomatoe
  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • 1 tablespoon of italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375.

Step 2:Heat frying pan on medium heat.

Step 3:Butter one side of both slices of bread and sprinkle with italian seasoning.

Step 4:Place one slice of bread butter slide first in the pan.

Step 5: Add one slice of cheese on the bread.

Step 6:Add 2 slices of turkey.

Step 7: Add tomatoe and spinach

Step 8: Add second slice of cheese

Step 9: Add other slice of bread

Step 10: Carefully flip sandwich and flatten with spatula.

Step 11: Cook until toast is golden brown on both sides.
