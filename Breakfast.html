Crustless Quiche

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 8 pieces of cooked bacon bits

Directions

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375.

Step 2: Whisk together eggs,milk,salt,pepper,and italian seasoning.

Step 3: Layer spinach and cheddar cheese on the bottom of a round pie baking dish .

Step 4: Pour egg mixture into the baking dish.

Step 5: Sprinkle with parmesan cheese and bacon bits.

Step 6: Bake for 30 minutes.

Step 7: Let cool for 10 minutes

Step 8: Serve and enjoy!
