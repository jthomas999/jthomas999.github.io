Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients

  • 15-20 Raw Jumbo Shrimp
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 cup corn, cooked
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup purple cabbage, chopped
  • 1 yellow onion, chopped
  • A pinch of salt and pepper
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 4 sliced avocados
  • 1 package Corn tortillas

Directions

Step 1:Heat olive oil in frying pan over medium heat.

Step 2: Peel shrimp and toss in taco seasoning.

Step 3: Cook shrimp for about three minutes on each side and set aside.

Step 4: In a medium bowl mix together corn, mayonnaise, purple cabbage, onion, salt pepper and lime juice, chill in the fridge.

Step 5: In a frying pan heat olive oil on medium heat.

Step 6:Place corn toritllas in the pan to warm for a few minutes

Step 7:Get the cabbage mixture from the fridge and place a few shrimp along with the mixure in a corn torilla.

Step 8: Top with slices of avocado and enjoy!
