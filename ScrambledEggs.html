Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • A handful of chopped spinach
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese
  • A sprinkle of parmesan cheese
  • 2 slices of chopped ham
  • 5-10 halved cherry tomatoes

Directions

Step 1:Cover bottom of a frying pan with olive oil and heat over medium heat.

Step 2: Whisk together eggs,salt,pepper,and italian seasoning.

Step 3: Add spinach, tomatoes and cheese to egg mixture.

Step 4: Heat ham pieces in the pan with olive oil.

Step 5: Pour egg mixture over the ham pieces in the frying pan.

Step 6: Cook for about 3 mintues before scrambling the eggs.

Step 7: After 3 minutes scramble the eggs with a spatula.

Step 8: Sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Step 9: Serve and enjoy!
