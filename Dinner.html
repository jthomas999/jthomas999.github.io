Shrimp Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 10-15 raw shrimp
  • 1 jar prego homestlye alfredo sauce
  • 1 box angel hair pasta
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon old bay seasoning

Step 1: Fill a pot with water. Bring the water to a boil and add the pasta. Cook the pasta for 10 minutes.

Step 2: Cover the bottom of a frying pan with olive oil and heat on medium heat.

Step 3: Remove the tails from the shirmp and toss in old bay seasoning. Place the shrimp in the pan with the olive oil. Cook the shrimp for 2 minutes per side.

Step 4: Pour the alfredo sauce into a small sauce pan and heat on low heat.

Step 5: Add the tomatoes to the sauce and continue to heat on low.

Step 6: Drain the pasta and return the pasta to the pot.

Step 7: Add the sauce and tomatoes to the pasta and stir.

Step 8: Add the shrimp to the pasta, sauce and tomatoes and stir.

Step 9: Let cool for 2 minutes.

Step 10: Serve and enjoy!
