Shrimp Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 10-15 raw shrimp
  • 1 jar prego homestlye alfredo sauce
  • 1 box angel hair pasta
  • 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon old bay seasoning
