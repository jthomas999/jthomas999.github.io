Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter shortening
  • 1 1/4 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions

Step 1:Preheat oven to 375.

Step 2:Mix together shortening, brown sugar, milk and vanilla in large bowl with mixer on medium speed until well blended.Mix in the egg.

Step 3:Combine flour,salt and baking soda in a seperate bowl.

Step 4:Mix flour mixure into shortening mixture with an electric mixer.

Step 5:Stir in chocolate chips.

Step 6: Drop about 1-inch balls of cookie dough a few inches apart on a cookie sheet.

Step 7: Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.
