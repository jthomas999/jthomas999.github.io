Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • A handful of chopped spinach
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 2 teaspoons italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheddar cheese
  • A sprinkle of parmesan cheese
  • 2 slices of chopped ham, chopped

Directions

Step 1:Cover bottom of a frying pan with olive oil and heat over medium heat.

Step 2: Whisk together eggs,salt,pepper,garlic salt and italian seasoning.

Step 3: Add spinach, ham pieces, and cheddar cheese to egg mixture.

Step 4: Cover the bottom of the frying pan with the egg mixturre.

Step 5: Cook egg mixture for about 5 minutes.

Step 6: Sprinkle egg mixture with parmesan cheese.

Step 7: Carefully fold Omelette. Flip and cook for another few minutes.

Step 8: Serve and enjoy!
